Lesson 1 - Path to conversion

BRANDED CONVERSION VIDEOS

– KEY TAKEAWAYS –

Point 1 - Build a brand character

Your brand character will present the problem and then your product or service as the solution to that problem. They must have two characteristics to be successful:
Empathy - Understands what your potential customers are going through
Authority - Helps establish trust and credibility. Authority should come from the credible information that your character provides.

Point 2 - Build a brand voice

While not the only option, comedy is one very effective way to build your brand's voice. Generally speaking, humor is the most sharable content on the internet, and this means that your marketing dollars can be more effective because you don't pay for the views that come from shared content. If, for example, 20% of your views come from social sharing, your ad dollars are now 20% more effective.

Point 3 - Build a brand universe

Creating a brand universe where your character(s) can live is the key to bringing the different aspects, metaphors, product demos, and other elements of your sale together, and making it appropriate.
