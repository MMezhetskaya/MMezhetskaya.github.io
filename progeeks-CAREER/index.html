We are looking for a Headhunter to join our team and help us fill our open positions with qualified candidates. Headhunter responsibilities include defining hiring criteria, sourcing potential candidates on social media and reaching out to prospects via email or phone. To be successful in this role, you should have excellent communication skills to interact with candidates and internal teams.

Job

  • Coordinate with managing directors and our clients to define necessary requirements for open roles in outsourcing positions.
  • Send recruiting emails to passive candidates using our database, other open sources and follow up when necessary.
  • Browse resume databases and portfolio sites.
  • Join social media groups and professional networks and interact with potential candidates.
  • Advertise open positions to external networks.
  • Ask for referrals from current employees, acquaintances and industry professionals.

Qualifications

  • Proven work experience as a Headhunter, Recruiter or similar in IT market.
  • Advanced knowledge of sourcing techniques (including Boolean search).
  • Hands-on experience with sourcing tools (e.g. resume databases and portfolio sites).
  • Familiarity with HR databases and Applicant Tracking Systems (ATSs).
